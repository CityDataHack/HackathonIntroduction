Tips
"Hack" + "Marathon" = "Hackathon" Despite what the hollywood movies have you believe hack is to build not to break
Different skills come together
High risk items first Timebox everything Breadth or Depth
Pivot is powerful and dangerous
Work together as a team Be transparent Treat the 2 days like a sprint Have hourly standups Keep things simple Divide and share work
Presentation tips Description the problem Explain the challenges you had Tell the story about your journey and paint a picture You want to take the people on your journey with you
Digitise all your notes and diagrams Take photos and upload them Think of people who are not only physically here Remote people may want to contribute now or in the future
Make the most of the event The more you put in the more you will get out Have fun and if you are not tired by the end you didnt put enough effort in
